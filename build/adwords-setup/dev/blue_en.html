How it works

  • 1. Lead Generation

    Car buyers create Demand by letting us know Who they are, What they want, Where they want to find it, When they need it by and Why they're buying it.

  • 2. We help Straighten Things Out

    Sometimes it's hard for buyers to qualify what they're looking for, making it very difficult for dealers to participate. So to get things back on track, we may send a sampling of available cars so buyers can qualify by example.

  • 3. We Publish

    We publish their Demand - not their email addresses, to our subscribing car dealers asking if they have Supply on hand AND can they offer some incentive for the buyer to contact them directly - like an unadvertised deal. We also agree on a Finder's Fee on resulting sales.

  • 4. Dealers Propose

    Interested car dealers submit their Proposals and contact information to us and we forward them to the car buyers. (Dealers are free to publish their email addresses on their own accord.)

  • 5. Self-correcting

    Buyers can Rate Proposals. Dealers can respond by proposing another deal and even Rate buyers too!

  • 6. It's As Easy As ABC (Always Be Closing)

    Buyers reach out to dealers & close the deal.

    About

    • Problem

      We believe that car buyers are already going to spend enough money. Adding more time & energy trying to find the right dealer, with the right car, for the right price, can be maddening! So that's why we created a sales lead co-op for local car dealers.

    • Solution

      Our objective is to shorten the sales cycle for both car buyers and car dealers, reducing everyone's cost of acquisition. We do not compete with dealers' Internet strategies, we actually complement them by directing our traffic to the dealers' web page of choice.


      Our aim is to level the playing field by enabling car dealers to compete on product, service and value - not search engine marketing budgets.

    • From the car buyer's point-of-view...

      It's as if they Googled for the used car they want to buy and actually called (connected with) every dealer in the search results - not just the top 5, or, 6, all at the same time!

    • From the car dealer's point-of-view...

      It's as if they were able to offer these qualified leads something unique, something that they wouldn't advertise on the web, something that would encourage them to call, or, visit the sales associate. It could be a better price but, it could also be something more immediate like getting a free car wash, a coffee mug, or, simply a really nice espresso when they come in to discuss their needs.

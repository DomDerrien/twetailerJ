How it works (it's a matter of Supply & Demand)
1. You Create

You create Demand by letting us know Who you are, What you want, Where you'd like to find it, When you need it by and Why you're buying it.

2. We Publish

We publish your Demand - not your email addresses, to our subscribing car dealers asking if they have Supply on hand.

3. Dealers Propose

Interested car dealers submit their Proposals and contact information to us and we forward them to you.

4. You Rate

You're then free to Rate those Proposals and contact the dealers to follow-up. BTW, car dealers can Rate you too!.

More people trust us because we're open and we're open because more people trust us

In order to assure the quality of our service and to better the community, we are currently looking to share our anonymous Rating data with consumer groups and car brands. In addition, should something go wrong, we can even facilitate filing a report with the appropriate organization.

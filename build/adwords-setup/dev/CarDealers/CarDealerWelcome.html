How it works (it's a matter of Supply & Demand)
1. Lead Generation

Car buyers create Demand by letting us know Who they are, What they want, Where they'd like to find it, When they need it by and Why they're buying it.

2. We Publish

We publish their Demand - not their email addresses, to our subscribing car dealers asking if they have Supply on hand.

3. Dealers Propose

Interested car dealers submit their Proposals and contact information to us and we forward them to the car buyers.

4. Car Buyers Rate

Car buyers are then free to Rate those Proposals and contact the dealers to follow-up. BTW, car dealers can Rate car buyers too!.

More people trust us because we're open and we're open because more people trust us

In order to assure the quality of our service and to better the community, we are currently looking to share our anonymous Rating data with consumer groups and car brands. In addition, should something go wrong, we can even facilitate filing a report with the appropriate organization.

About

AnotherSocialEconomy believes that car buyers are already going to spend enough money. Adding more time & energy trying to find the right dealer, with the right car, for the right price, can be maddening! So that's why we created a sales lead co-op for local car dealers.

Our aim is to level the playing field by enabling car dealers to compete on product, service and value - not search engine marketing budgets.

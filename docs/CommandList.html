Actions

help
[]: send generic message
[keyword]: send message associated with the keyword
cancel
[demandKey]: cancel demand (restriction: owner), with notification of the cancellation to associated proposal owners
[proposalKey]: cancel proposal (restriction: owner)
close
[demandKey]: confirm the delivery of the product (restriction: owner)
[proposalKey]: confirm the delivery of the product (restriction: owner)
confirm
[proposalKey]: select one proposal (restriction: demand.owner), with the notification to the proposal owner with consumer information
decline
proposalKey]: refuse one proposal (restriction: demand.owner), with the notification to the proposal owner
demand
[tags, ...]: create a demand
[demandKey, tags, ...]: update a demand (restriction: owner)
list
[]: list all demands (restriction: limited to owner's demand, in the state {invalid, published, confirmed})
[demandKey]: list one demand (restriction: owner or one associated proposal owner)
[proposalKey]: list one proposal (restriction: owner or the associated demand owner)
[storeKey]: list one store information
propose
[tags, ...]: create a proposal (restriction: only retailers can propose for demands that has been sent to them or their store)
[proposalKey, tags, ...]: update one proposal (restriction: owner or another retailer from the same store)
supply
tags]: add supplied tags to the retailer account
wish
[tags, ...]: create a wish
[wishKey, tags, ...]: update one wish (restriction: owner)
